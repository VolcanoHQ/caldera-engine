How it works

A browser studio puts the author in the director's chair — not a black box.

01 · Upload

Drop in a manuscript

EPUB or plain text. Deterministic-first analysis finds parts, chapters, scenes, and every line of dialogue — zero LLM calls on clean manuscripts.

02 · Review

Correct anything

A review console for speaker attribution and scene boundaries. Preview any tier on a single scene before committing.

03 · Direct

Mix like a director

Tier 3's multi-lane scene mixer: voice, music, ambience, and SFX on a timeline, with per-asset regeneration — new take, or your own prompt.

04 · Render

One button

A chaptered, mastered M4B with a per-line timing manifest. Revisions are instant and free.

A consent-based voice marketplace

Actors get cast, not copied

Voice actors record one ~15-minute session, publish a licensed voice with SHA-256 consent provenance, and get paid per use.

Authors audition voices by description and give every character a distinct, licensed performance.

Measured, not promised

Every accuracy claim has a denominator: 99.2% dialogue attribution against human-cleaned gold references, ≤0.31% text contamination, regression-tested after every change.

Les Misérables — 50 parts, 498 chapters, 723 scenes — parses fully deterministically.
